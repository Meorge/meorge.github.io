Sneaksters

Unity

Every heist needs a good team!

Important note: Sneaksters is still in development. Screenshots provided here may not reflect the current or final state of the game.

In Sneaksters, a group is split into two different "sides", and must communicate in order to locate, nab, and escape with treasure.

The Thieves are in the thick of the action. Their job is to navigate through a labyrinth, grab treasures, and get them safely back to home base without being caught by guards. However, their tool-set is quite limited; they play from a first-person view and have no way to stop the guards or even open closed doors.

The Eyes in the Sky watch over everything from above. They can see the positions of guards and treasure, as well as Thieves (as long as they're within a Visibility Beacon). They can also open and close doors for the Thieves, but can't interact with the environment more directly than that.

Communication is vital for a successful mission. The Eyes in the Sky must guide the Thieves to the treasure and keep them safe from guards, and the Thieves must give the Eyes in the Sky important information that they may not have.

Sneaksters is currently in public pre-alpha. In addition to the original English, the game has been translated by the community into Spanish, German, French, Korean, and Japanese.
